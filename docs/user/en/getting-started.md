# Getting Started

Welcome to **FamilyHub** — your family life, one place! This guide shows you how to create an account, join your family, and put the app on your phone.

## Create an account

1. Open FamilyHub in your browser (you'll get the address from whoever set up FamilyHub for your family).
2. Tap **Sign up**.
3. Enter **Your name**, your **Email**, and a **Password** (at least 8 characters).
4. Tap **Sign up** — that's your account done.

> **Note:** Every family member needs their own account with their own email address.

## Create or join a family

Right after signing up you'll see "Almost there!" — you have two options:

### Create a new family

First one from your family here?

1. Choose **Create a new family**.
2. Enter a **Family name**, e.g. "The Smiths".
3. Tap **Create**.

Afterwards you'll find your family's **Invite code** in **Settings** — share it with the rest of the family.

### Join a family

Someone in your family already created one?

1. Ask them for the **Invite code** (found under **Settings** → **Family**).
2. Choose **Join a family**.
3. Enter the code and confirm.

You'll immediately see all shared tasks, events, and mind maps.

## Signing in and out

- **Sign in:** Open FamilyHub, enter your **Email** and **Password**, and tap **Sign in**. You stay signed in until you sign out.
- **Sign out:** Tap **Sign out** in the menu.

## Switching the language

FamilyHub is available in German and English:

1. Open **Settings**.
2. Under **Language**, choose **German** or **English**.
3. Tap **Save**.

See [Settings](settings.md) for details.

## Install FamilyHub on your phone

FamilyHub is a web app you can add to your home screen like a regular app — no app store needed.

### iPhone / iPad (Safari)

1. Open FamilyHub in **Safari**.
2. Tap the **Share icon** (square with an arrow pointing up).
3. Choose **"Add to Home Screen"**.
4. Tap **Add**.

### Android (Chrome)

1. Open FamilyHub in **Chrome**.
2. Tap the **three-dot menu** in the top right.
3. Choose **"Add to Home screen"** (or "Install app").

> **Tip:** FamilyHub then launches straight from your home screen — full screen, no browser bars. On the phone you navigate with the tab bar at the bottom: **Dashboard**, **Tasks**, **Calendar**, **Mind maps**, and **Settings**.
