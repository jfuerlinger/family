# Calendar

The **Calendar** shows all of your family's events in one place: doctor's appointments, soccer practice, birthdays, vacations. Every family member sees the same calendar and can add events.

## Navigating the month

- The calendar opens in the **month view** of the current month.
- Use the **arrows** to go one month back or forward.
- Tap **Today** to jump back to the current day at any time.
- Each day shows small colored entries for that day's events.

## The day agenda

Tap a day to open its **day agenda**: all events of that day listed with time, location, and description. From here you can open and edit events, or create a new one for exactly that day.

## Creating an event

1. Open the **Calendar** and tap the day you want (or tap **Add**).
2. Enter a **title**, e.g. "Dentist — Lena".
3. Choose the kind of event:
   - **All-day** — for birthdays, holidays, or school breaks, no time needed.
   - **Timed** — pick a start time and optionally an end, e.g. 3:00–4:30 pm.
4. Optionally add:
   - **Location** — e.g. "Dr. Huber's office"
   - **Description** — e.g. "Bring the insurance card"
   - **Color** — for a clearer overview, e.g. blue for sports, red for medical
5. Tap **Save**.

> **Tip:** Agree on colors as a family — one color per person or per topic. That way one glance at the month tells you what's coming.

## Editing or deleting an event

1. Tap the event (in the month view or the day agenda).
2. Choose **Edit**, change what you need, and tap **Save**.
3. Or choose **Delete** to remove the event. There's a confirmation prompt — deleted events can't be restored.

## Events on the Dashboard

The **Dashboard** shows **Events today** and **Upcoming events** — so the moment you open the app you know what's happening today and over the next few days.

> **Note:** Recurring events (e.g. "practice every Tuesday") aren't supported yet. For now, create those events individually.
