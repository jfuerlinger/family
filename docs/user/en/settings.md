# Settings

**Settings** is where you manage your profile, the language, and everything about your family.

## Profile

In the **Profile** section you can change your name:

1. Open **Settings**.
2. Enter the name you want under **Your name**.
3. Tap **Save** — "Saved" appears briefly.

Your name shows up throughout the app, e.g. on assigned tasks and in the member list.

> **Note:** Email address and password can't be changed yourself at the moment.

## Language

FamilyHub speaks **German** and **English**:

1. Choose your language under **Language**.
2. Tap **Save**.

The app switches immediately. The setting is personal — every family member picks their own language.

## Family

### Invite code

In the **Family** section you'll find your **Invite code**. As the app puts it: "Share this code so others can join your family."

1. Tap **Copy** — "Copied!" appears.
2. Send the code to the new family member.
3. They sign up, choose **Join a family**, and enter the code.

> **Tip:** Treat the invite code like a house key — anyone who has it can see all of your family's tasks, events, and mind maps. Only share it with people who truly belong.

### Members

Under **Members** you see everyone in your family along with their role:

- **Admin** — the person who created the family
- **Member** — everyone who joined via invite code

Right now the role is informational only — admins and members can do the same things in the app.

## Signing out

Use **Sign out** in the menu to sign out of your account — for example on a shared device.
