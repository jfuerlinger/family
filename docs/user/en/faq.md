# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## I forgot my password. Now what?

There's no "forgot password" feature yet, unfortunately. Contact the person who runs FamilyHub for you (the admin): they can recreate your account. Your shared family data — tasks, events, mind maps — is safe either way, because it belongs to the family, not to your account.

## Where is our data stored? Who can see it?

FamilyHub is **self-hosted**: your data lives exclusively on the server that you (or your family's admin) run — no third-party provider, no big-cloud companies, no ads. Only the members of your family can see your data.

## Can I be in more than one family?

No — each account currently belongs to exactly **one** family. If you need to cover blended-family setups, for now you'll need a second account with a different email address.

## How do I invite someone to our family?

Open **Settings** → **Family** and copy the **Invite code**. The new person signs up, chooses **Join a family**, and enters the code. Done! See [Getting started](getting-started.md) for details.

## Can I remove a family member?

There's no in-app way to remove members yet. Until there is, the person running the server can remove a member directly in the database.

## Is there an iPhone or Android app?

FamilyHub works on any phone in the browser and can be **added to your home screen** — it then feels like a regular app (see [Getting started](getting-started.md)). For iPhone and iPad there's additionally a native app shell that your family's admin can install.

## Does FamilyHub work offline?

No. FamilyHub needs a connection to your server — after all, everyone in the family should always see the same up-to-date information. Without internet (or if the server is unreachable), the app can't be used.

## Do I get reminders or notifications?

Push notifications and event reminders don't exist yet. But the **Dashboard** instantly shows you what's **Due today** or **Overdue**, plus your **Events today**, every time you open the app.

## Can everyone in the family see everything?

Yes. All lists, tasks, events, and mind maps are visible and editable for the whole family. There are deliberately no private lists or events — FamilyHub is the shared place for your shared things.

## Which languages does FamilyHub support?

**German** and **English**. Every family member picks their own language under **Settings** → **Language**.
